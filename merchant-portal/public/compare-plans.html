Common questions
Everything you need to know before you start
Lite has no free trial — you pay from day one at ₹599/month. Growth offers a 30-day free trial with no credit card required upfront. Your payment method is collected at day 21 to ensure no interruption. On Pro, payment details are agreed after your demo call.
Custom card design is free for all merchants during the initial launch period. This means our team will design a branded loyalty card for your shop at no charge. After the promotional period, custom design will be available at ₹999 per card as a one-time fee.
Yes, at any time. Upgrades are prorated — you pay the difference for the remaining days in your billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Your data and customer stamps are always preserved.
If you cancel, your account enters a 30-day dormant state where your customers' stamp data is fully preserved. Reactivate any time within 30 days and pick up exactly where you left off. After 30 days data is archived for 12 months.
Each plan includes a base outlet. Additional outlets are +₹599/mo on Lite, +₹999/mo on Growth, and +₹799/mo on Pro (up to 10 outlets). Beyond 10 outlets on Pro, contact us for custom pricing. Your Subscription page shows your exact monthly total.
All prices shown are exclusive of 18% GST. GST is added at checkout and a proper GST invoice is automatically generated and emailed on every billing date.
An active customer is any unique consumer who has scanned at your outlet at least once in the last 30 days. On Lite, this is capped at 100 — a natural limit for small single-outlet shops. Growth and Pro have no cap.
